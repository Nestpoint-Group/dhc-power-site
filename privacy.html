§ 01 Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how DHC Power, Inc. ("DHC Power," "we," "us," or "our") handles information collected through this website (the "Site"). It does not govern information collected offline, through direct contractual engagements, or through channels other than the Site.

By using the Site, you acknowledge the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the Site.

§ 02 Information we collect

Information you provide

When you contact us — for example, through our contact form — we collect the information you choose to submit, such as your name, organization, email address, and the contents of your message.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, we (and our service providers) may automatically collect limited technical data, such as IP address, browser and device type, pages viewed, referring URLs, and timestamps. This is used for security, diagnostics, and understanding aggregate usage.

What we ask you not to submit

This Site is informational. Please do not submit sensitive personal information, classified material, or export-controlled technical data through the Site. See our Terms of Use regarding export-controlled information.

§ 03 How we use information

We use information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and evaluate potential programs, partnerships, or investment conversations;
  • Operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Site;
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or security incidents;
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations and enforce our terms.

We do not sell personal information, and we do not use the contents of inquiries for advertising.

§ 04 How we share information

We share information only as needed:

  • Service providers who host the Site or process inquiries on our behalf, under appropriate confidentiality obligations;
  • Legal and safety — where required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and property;
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, financing, acquisition, or similar transaction;
  • With your direction or consent.

The Site may use cookies or similar technologies that are strictly necessary for it to function, and — where applicable — analytics to understand aggregate usage. § 07 Data security

We maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

§ 08 Your choices & rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal information, or to opt out of certain processing. For example, residents of California (CCPA/CPRA) and the EEA/UK (GDPR) may have specific rights.

To make a request, contact us through our contact form or at contact@dhcpower.com. We will respond consistent with applicable law and may need to verify your identity.

§ 09 International users

DHC Power is based in the United States, and information we collect may be processed in the United States. If you access the Site from outside the United States, you understand your information may be transferred to and processed in a country with different data-protection laws than your own.

§ 10 Children's privacy

The Site is intended for a business and professional audience and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can delete it.

§ 11 Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice. Your continued use of the Site after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
