Design System

The visual companion to docs/DESIGN_SYSTEM.md. Tactical / defense-tech: charcoal and off-white do the work; brand green is a scalpel.

§ 00  /  THE GREEN RULE

Green ≤ ~5–8% of any page.

Brand green (#56DB56) is permitted only on: primary CTA buttons, status / live indicator dots, link hover + underline states, and form focus borders. Never on stat values, headings, eyebrows, card hovers, corner brackets, or large fills. When in doubt, don't use green.
